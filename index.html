Marksheet about:
American (Marksheet) A document detailing the marks or grades obtained by a member in various examinations during his educational life. It usually includes the results obtained by the student during a particular period (eg: Semester Annual Examination), number of marks and total marks.
  • Information that a marksheet usually contains:
    1. Student Name and Roll Number: This identifies the student.
    2. Exam Name and Date: The date when the exam was conducted is mentioned.
    3. List of Subjects: Details of the examination given in any subject or course.
    4. Marks or Grades Obtained: Marks or grades obtained for each subject.
    5. Total Marks: Total marks or score of all subjects.
    6. Percentage or CGPA: If a student is reviewed in the grade system, the Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) or percentage is also given.
    7. Special Comments (if any): Any special comments related to the test results.

It is usually published by educational institutions (schools, colleges, universities) and is considered as an important record of the student's educational life.
